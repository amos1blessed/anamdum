CORPORATE SAFETY & ENVIRONMENT POLICY:

Safety is everyone’s business and society people in authority to protect their subordinates from harms.
Workers are responsible for cooperation and taking measures to safeguard themselves and their fellow workers.
The management of Anam-dum Nigeria Limited fell that health and safety are not only a moral and legal requirement,
but we are a company who care for one another.

Our management, supervisors, employees, subcontractors and vendors are accountable to achieve our health and safety goals on our project sites, in our offices and traveling on business by;
-conforming to environment impact assessment (EIA) guideline
-motivating proactive actions and precautions to deter substandard safety practice
-Hiring competent and responsible employee, subcontractors and vendors.
-operating and maintaining tools and equipment in a safe and responsible manner and for the uses they were design for.
-preserving to identify and control hazardous conditions in our workplace environment.

Consistently applying and enforcing the health and safety programme rules and ensuring employees receive adequate
training in the rules, regulations, practice and procedures before they commence work.

Management’s responsibilities includes leadership, administration and fostering participation for an effective and
continually improving health and safety program conforming to the best practices of organizations in the marine,
water and waste water treatment business sector.

Supervisors are responsible for development attitudes of their subordinates, ensuring safe operations and
continuous attentiveness to employee’s active duties to ensure all task are performed with the utmost regards
for the safety and health of all personnel.

Employees are responsible for their genuine cooperation with all health and safety programs,
full compliance with the rules and regulations and continued safe practices while performing their duties.
